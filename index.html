About

I enjoy building web products that combine data analytics with delightful design. I studied software engineering and web development at Thinkful and management science at Stanford. I previously worked in product marketing, where I helped launch products and drive towards product-market fit. As a marketer, I discovered my enthusiasm for web development and design, and am re-orienting my career to follow my passions.

Beyond web development and product marketing, my main interest is finance, particularly asset management, alterative investments, and blockchain technology. I want to see a world where sophisticated finance is accessible to everyone through thoughtful data science and design. I am looking to join a sociable team of developers who value communication, mentorship, and a willingness to learn. Please contact me to discuss any of these projects:

  • Web Development

  • Modern design systems (BEM, Atomic, Styled Components)
