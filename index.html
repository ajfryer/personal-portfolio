DFunds. Exploration of big data tables, charts, and statistical models.

About

Dfunds helps seasoned crypto investors save time, money, and effort analyzing hundreds of investment funds running on the Ethereum blockchain. — DFunds wants to bring core features of Bloomberg, Morningstar, and ETFDb to the new and growing crypto native fund industry.

Scope

Sitemapping: 3 core product pages • 1. Industry Overview • 2. Fund Screener and • 3. Fund Profiles.

UX strategy: 2 Personas — 1. Ryder the Relative Investor and 2. April the Absolute Investor

UI prototyping: focuses on Progressiveness of tables, charts, and models for high-dimensional data

Frontend: React Typescript components Emotion Css-In-Js styles. Main technical risk is table virtualization.
